About Us

KHope Community Brass Band is a small community brass band that was started by Brian Wambi a single parented child who learned music through brass band help of a Catholic Rev father Wijnand Huys and late Mugulusi Paul From Mbiko Brass Band and when he saw that the community he was staying in also had same challanges that he faced he started up a brass band for the community with the little he had to help suppor orphans and vulnerable children.For him he believes that through music education and performance opportunities we can help children develop skills, teamwork, self-belief and in the future be Good musicians or even engineers like him through the help given.

    ❤️Why This Project Matters ❤️
  • Many children in our community face:
  • Loss of parental care
  • Poverty and limited access to education
  • Lack of safe, structured extracurricular activities
        • Without support, talent often goes undeveloped and children are exposed to negative influences so Your support helps transform **raw potential into purpose**

Our Program

  • Free brass instrument training
  • Weekly rehearsals and mentorship
  • Community and church performances
  • Life skills and teamwork development
